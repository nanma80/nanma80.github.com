On the Rubik's Cube you may twist a layer. But on "Mirror +" you may mirror a layer to swap opposite edges; on "Mirror X" you may mirror a layer diagonally to swap a pair of corners. When you are really ready, check out the last puzzle allowing all kinds of moves!

If you can solve them, try different scrambles to make sure you haven't missed the exotic phenomena that are forbidden on the Rubik's Cube.

Nan Ma, 12/15/2012
